About Us

We are a small cafe founded out of a passion for exceptional Ethiopian coffee. Our beans are sourced directly from smallholder farmers in Ethiopia, the birthplace of coffee. We self-roast the beans on-site in small batches to bring out their best flavors. The cafe offers a cozy space to enjoy traditional Ethiopian coffee ceremonies, espressos, pour-overs, and more. Our goal is to provide an authentic Ethiopian coffee experience from bean to cup, as well as educate people on the origins and artistry of this remarkable coffee tradition. We invite you to join us and savor Ethiopia's gift to the world - incredible coffee!
